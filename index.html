Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI personalize stories for my child?

+

Our AI uses your child's name, favorite toys, characters, and interests to create unique stories. Simply input these details in the app, and our AI will weave them into magical bedtime adventures that feel specially crafted for your little one.

Can I record multiple family members' voices?

+

Yes! You can record voices from mom, dad, grandparents, or any family member. The app allows you to create multiple voice profiles, so your child can choose who narrates their story each night.

Is the app safe for children?

+

Absolutely! Storio is designed with child safety in mind. All stories are age-appropriate, there are no ads, and we use secure, encrypted storage for your family's voice recordings and personal information.

How many stories can I create?

+

You can create unlimited stories! Our AI generates new content every time, so your child will never hear the same story twice. Each story is unique and personalized to their current interests.

Can I use the app offline?

+

Yes! Once you've created and downloaded stories, you can listen to them offline. Perfect for bedtime routines, car rides, or when you're away from internet connectivity.

What age range is Storio designed for?

+

Storio is perfect for children ages 2-10. The AI adjusts story complexity based on your child's age, ensuring the content is engaging and appropriate for their developmental stage.
